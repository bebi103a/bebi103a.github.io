24. The paper of the future
===========================

`Griffin Chure <https://gchure.github.io>`_ gave a guest lecture on the paper of the future and templates he built for publishing your work with interactive websites.


Links to resources
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- Griffin created a template for a website you can clone and use for your own publications. You can access it `here <https://github.com/gchure/reproducible_website>`_.
- Don't forget that the `wiki of the repository <https://github.com/gchure/reproducible_website/wiki>`_ has usage instructions.
