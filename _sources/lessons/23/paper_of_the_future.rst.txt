23. The paper of the future
===========================

`Griffin Chure <https://gchure.github.io>`_ will give a guest lecture on the paper of the future and templates he built for publishing your work with interactive websites. He showed a few examples of papers-of-the-future that he has published. You can find links to those paper from `his website <https://gchure.github.io/research/>`_. He also showed `his PhD thesis at Caltech <https://gchure.github.io/phd/>`_, and you can check out the public repository from which it was generated `on GitHub <https://github.com/gchure/phd>`_. You can even use this repo as a template for preparing your own thesis!




Links to resources
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

- Griffin created a template for a website you can clone and use for your own publications. You can access it `here <https://github.com/gchure/reproducible_website>`_.
- Don't forget that the `wiki of the repository <https://github.com/gchure/reproducible_website/wiki>`_ has usage instructions.
