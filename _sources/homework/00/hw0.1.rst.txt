Homework 0.1: Configure your team (0 pts)
------------------------------------------

One, and only one, member from your team, please email the course staff the following information.

1. The names of the team members.
2. The GitHub IDs of the team members. (This is not your GitHub account number, but is the user names. E.g., mine is ``justinbois``.)
3. Which of the Monday sections you will be joining.
4. A link to the recurring Zoom meeting your team will use for the class.

The staff email addresses are available from the :ref:`main page of the course<
People>`.
