Homework 0.1: Configure your team (0 pts)
------------------------------------------

One, and only one, member from your team, please email the course staff (all TAs and the instructor) the following information.

1. The names of the team members.
2. The GitHub usernames of the team members. (This is not your GitHub account number, but is your username. E.g., mine is ``justinbois``.)
3. Which of the Monday sections you are in.

The staff email addresses are available from the :ref:`main page of the course<
People>`.
